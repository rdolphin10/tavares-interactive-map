IMAGE GUIDELINES:
-----------------
- Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP (you can change the extension if needed)
- Recommended width: 300-800 pixels (popup displays at max 400px wide)
- Keep file size under 500KB for fast loading
- Any aspect ratio works - the popup will scale automatically

TIPS:
-----
- You can work on these one at a time or in batches
- The placeholders will display on the map until you replace them
- You can test the map after replacing each image to see how it looks
- If you don't have an image for a business yet, just leave the placeholder

TESTING:
--------
After replacing images, open index.html in a browser and:
1. Click on a business marker
2. The popup should show your new image
3. Verify it scales properly and looks clean
